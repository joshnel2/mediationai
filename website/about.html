About MediationAI

Democratizing justice through AI-powered, lightning-fast dispute resolution.

Mission

MediationAI exists to remove the friction, cost and complexity of traditional dispute resolution. We leverage frontier language models, legal ontologies and smart escrow to resolve disagreements in minutes—not months—so people and businesses can focus on what matters.

Vision

We envision a world where fair outcomes are only a few taps away, no matter your income, jurisdiction or background. By 2030, our goal is to empower 100 million users to settle conflicts peacefully, without ever stepping inside a courtroom.
